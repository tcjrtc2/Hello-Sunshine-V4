Hello Sunshine

A deep experiment where older and younger people are paired for four weeks to prove that the generational disconnect is a myth.

Why Hello Sunshine?

In a world where we're told generations can't connect, Hello Sunshine tests a simple hypothesis: that small, intentional interactions can reduce loneliness and spark genuine relationships across age divides.

This isn't about forced friendship or charity. It's about showing up, being present, and discovering that connection is possible when we actually try.

Brené Brown - “Connection is why we’re here; it is what gives purpose and meaning to our lives."
